How it works
Three steps. No setup.
01 —
Connect to WiFi
Open DataVault when you're on any WiFi network — at home, a café, your university. The app begins collecting data silently in the background.
02 —
Data gets saved
Up to 300 MB is stored locally on your device. Browse sites, cache content — it all counts. Your vault fills up automatically.
03 —
Use it offline
No signal? Low bars? Doesn't matter. Your 300 MB vault works for 24 hours — stream, browse, and load pages like you're still connected.
Why DataVault
Built from a real problem.
📡
Data is expensive
In Uzbekistan and most of Central Asia, mobile data costs 3–5× more per GB than Western Europe. Every megabyte matters.
🎮
WiFi isn't always available
You have WiFi at home or in a café — but not on the bus, at school, or in the street. DataVault bridges that gap.
⚡
Zero extra steps
No manual downloads, no content selection. Just connect to WiFi and DataVault handles the rest — completely in the background.
🔒
Stored on your device
Your data never leaves your phone. No cloud, no accounts required for the basic version. Private by design.
